Tea Cafe

The aim of the assignment was to develop a website for a tea cafe opening up. I set out to design a website that would invite people to visit the cafe while informing people of the various products they have. I started by looking into what the competitions websites look like, and what to focus on. The decision-making process, was then translated into the website user experience. I also got feedback on the user experience from other people who would ordinarily go to other cafe websites.
